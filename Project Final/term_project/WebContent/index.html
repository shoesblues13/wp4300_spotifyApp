Apollo - The Spotify Party Playlist App


Find a party: 


With Apollo, the burden is no longer on the party host to pick music for everyone to enjoy at parties and hangouts! Let your guests control what they hear!

  1. Party Master creates a new party by signing into Spotify here.
  2. Party Master gets a unique code to share with attendees.
  3. Attendees search here for their party using the code.
  4. Start adding songs! The Party Master's Spotify playlist is automatically updated with the added songs.
